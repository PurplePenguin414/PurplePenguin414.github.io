reetings class,

Welcome to CIS 2353!  We will be studying a lot about data structures (data types that act as containers) and algorithms (well-defined, ordered, finite sets of steps that solve problems).  

The course will be held both remotely and asynchronously:

  • Remote is related to location, and means that you don't come to campus, the park, my house, or a restaurant to receive instruction.  Instead, you learn at home or wherever you are connecting from.
  • Asynchronous is related to time, and means that I will not be holding sessions at a particular time at which you must show up on Zoom or Google Meet or Discord or anything like that.  Instead, I will upload pre-recorded videos containing material for the week.

So, since the course is asynchronous, the videos are pre-recorded.  Some (or many) videos may be recorded in a previous semester, but except for the syllabus - which is of course different, the material is the same. 

Any section/semester-specific information will be in this semester's syllabus and additional information will be provided as needed here on D2L.

I will communicate primarily through D2L.  Feel free to e-mail me questions you might have about course content.  In some cases, if there are multiple questions about the same topic, I may create a special recording or other materials to help make your life easier.
